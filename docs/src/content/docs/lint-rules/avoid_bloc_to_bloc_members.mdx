Avoid including `Bloc`s or `Cubit`s as members of other `Bloc`s or `Cubit`s.

## Rationale

While passing blocs to other blocs may be error free, it creates dependencies
between blocs.

See also
[Bloc-to-Bloc Communication](https://bloclibrary.dev/architecture/#bloc-to-bloc-communication)
